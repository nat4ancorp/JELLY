Your Wedding Party

You and your groom choose your own attendants, usually about six to nine months before the wedding. The formal wedding generally consists of Best Man, Maid or Matron of Honor, Bridesmaids, Groomsmen, Ring Bearer, Flower Girl and Ushers (Groomsmen may also serve as ushers). For a semi-formal wedding choose 2 to 6 attendants each, and informal choose one honor attendant with one extra bridesmaid or groomsman.
